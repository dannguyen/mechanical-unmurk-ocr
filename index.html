    mechanical-unmurk-ocr

    Murkily un-murk those murky documents

    For the OCRing of scanned documents where speed, accuracy, privacy and cost are all factors of your consideration.

    Done in Ruby, for now.

    External components:

    • Tesseract 3.0: To OCR the scanned files
    • Nokogiri: To parse the generated hOCR files
    • ImageMagick: To break the original scanned images into their component hOCR entities

    Synergestic proactive paradigms used to sound important:

    • Brute-force programming
    • Neural Networks
    • Crowdsourcing
    • AJAX/HTML5
    • The Cloud
    • Statistics
